Event Report :

The webinar on 'how to ace an internship' was a great success where the speaker explained the entire process of an internship in a nutshell.

Starting from the impact of internship on students to performing well in an internship, everything was utterly addressed with utmost perfection and precision by our respected speaker. The speaker explained how to get an internship, connect with officials and accomplish it. It was great to hear about the pitfalls of an internship and know about the common mistakes made by a student during an internship. It inspired students to feel more confident preparing mini-projects before applying to an internship. In short, smart work with consistent learning can be helpful to ace an internship.

Besides, various ideas, thoughts, strategies, and plans were stated which made us believe that no matter what, if you give your best efforts combined with smart work then nothing can stop you from acing an internship.

It was the honor of GirlScript Jamshedpur to have such an eminent speaker and a worthy audience for the event.
